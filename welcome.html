The SDSC College Archiving System is a centralized digital platform designed to securely store, manage, and preserve academic records and scholarly works from all college departments of St. Dominic Savio College. This system serves as an organized repository for theses, capstone projects, research papers, and other institutional documents essential to academic growth and continuity.

Developed to improve accessibility and efficiency, the archiving system allows students, faculty, and administrators to easily upload, search, and retrieve academic materials anytime and anywhere. By digitizing records, the system ensures long-term preservation, reduces physical storage limitations, and promotes responsible information management.

The SDSC College Archiving System supports transparency, collaboration, and knowledge sharing across departments, helping strengthen research culture and institutional memory. Through this platform, St. Dominic Savio College upholds its commitment to innovation, academic excellence, and effective digital transformation.
